COVID-19 vaccination doses administered in Germany as of 2021-07-07

Across all of Germany, 39.92% of inhabitants (33,202,521 out of 83,166,711 people) are fully vaccinated. 17.20% (14,302,749 people) are partially vaccinated but still need a second shot. Overall, 57.12% (47,505,270 people) received at least one vaccination. A total of 78,672,892 doses have been administered so far — that’s 88.81% of the 88,587,702 total doses that have been delivered to Germany as of 2021-07-04.

Note: For all charts on this page, “initial dose” refers to any first dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, or Oxford/AstraZeneca) only. “Final dose” refers to any dose that completes vaccination, i.e. either a dose of Johnson & Johnson, or a second dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, or Oxford/AstraZeneca).

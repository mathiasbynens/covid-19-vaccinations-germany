COVID-19 vaccination doses administered in Germany as of 2022-03-31

Across all of Germany, 75.94% of inhabitants (63,156,591 out of 83,166,711 people) are fully vaccinated. 58.65% of inhabitants (48,779,303 out of 83,166,711 people) have additionally received a booster vaccination. 0.59% (492,623 people) are partially vaccinated but still need a second shot. Overall, 76.50% (63,621,854 people) received at least one vaccination. A total of 171,998,342 doses have been administered so far — that’s 88.51% of the 194,327,670 total doses that have been delivered to Germany as of 2022-03-01.

Note: For all charts on this page, “initial dose” refers to any first dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, Oxford/AstraZeneca, or Novavax) only. “Final dose” refers to any dose that completes vaccination, i.e. either a dose of Johnson & Johnson, or a second dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, Oxford/AstraZeneca, or Novavax).

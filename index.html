COVID-19 vaccination doses administered in Germany as of 2021-10-06

Across all of Germany, 64.82% of inhabitants (53,907,281 out of 83,166,711 people) are fully vaccinated. 3.44% (2,861,787 people) are partially vaccinated but still need a second shot. Overall, 68.26% (56,769,068 people) received at least one vaccination. A total of 108,331,178 doses have been administered so far — that’s 92.72% of the 116,839,725 total doses that have been delivered to Germany as of 2021-09-19.

Note: For all charts on this page, “initial dose” refers to any first dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, or Oxford/AstraZeneca) only. “Final dose” refers to any dose that completes vaccination, i.e. either a dose of Johnson & Johnson, or a second dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, or Oxford/AstraZeneca).

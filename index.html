COVID-19 vaccination doses administered in Germany as of 2021-04-13

Across all of Germany, 16.31% of inhabitants received their first vaccination dose, and 6.15% received their second dose. A total of 18,684,873 doses have been administered so far — that’s 83.25% of the 22,444,875 total doses that have been delivered to Germany as of 2021-04-07. The current vaccination rate (7-day average) is 515,687 doses per day.

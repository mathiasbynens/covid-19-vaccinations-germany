COVID-19 vaccination doses administered in Germany as of 2021-02-11

Across all of Germany, 2.99% of inhabitants received their first vaccination dose, and 1.42% received their second dose. A total of 3,669,148 doses have been administered so far — that’s 79.91% of the 4,591,665 total doses that have been delivered to Germany so far. The current vaccination rate (7-day average) is 117,304 doses per day.

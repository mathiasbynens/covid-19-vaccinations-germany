COVID-19 vaccination doses administered in Germany as of 2021-11-24

Across all of Germany, 68.10% of inhabitants (56,637,852 out of 83,166,711 people) are fully vaccinated. 2.59% (2,153,437 people) are partially vaccinated but still need a second shot. Overall, 70.69% (58,791,289 people) received at least one vaccination. A total of 118,672,955 doses have been administered so far — that’s 94.41% of the 125,698,086 total doses that have been delivered to Germany as of 2021-11-16.

Note: For all charts on this page, “initial dose” refers to any first dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, or Oxford/AstraZeneca) only. “Final dose” refers to any dose that completes vaccination, i.e. either a dose of Johnson & Johnson, or a second dose of a two-dose vaccine (i.e. Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna, or Oxford/AstraZeneca).
